Plastik Ipatalsik, a campaign to regulate Single-Use Plastic.
Plastik Ipatalsik is a campaign dedicated to regulate the use of harmful single-use plastics. These plastics are not only harmful to the environment but also to marine life.

A few facts about plastic,
and why it is harmful.

Since the 1950s, around 8.3 billion tons of plastic have been produced worldwide.

According to a report from the Guardian, an estimated 8.3 billion tons of plastic have been produced since the 1950s — that’s equivalent to the weight of more than 800,000 Eiffel Towers.


And only 9% of it has been recycled.
